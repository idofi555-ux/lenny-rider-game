## How to Play

1. **Hold** anywhere on screen (or press Space) to accelerate
2. **Release** while in the air to flip your car
3. **Land flat** on your wheels - landing at an angle will crash!
4. **Collect coins** for bonus points
5. **Complete challenges** to unlock new ones
